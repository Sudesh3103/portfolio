Achievement 1

Zonal Table Tennis Winner

Introduction:
Participating in the Zonal Table Tennis Championship organized by Shivaji University Kolhapur on November 28th was a defining moment in my sporting journey. This competition provided a platform to showcase not only my table tennis skills but also my dedication and passion for the sport.

Achievement:
Emerging as the winner of the Zonal Table Tennis Championship was a remarkable achievement that highlighted my commitment to excellence. Competing against skilled opponents from various colleages, we managed to secure the first place through relentless training, strategic gameplay, and unwavering determination.

Key Moment:
The tournament was a roller-coaster of intense matches, each demanding peak performance. One of the most memorable moments was a nail-biting semifinal match that pushed our skills to the limit. Facing a tough adversary, we summoned our mental strength and adaptability, ultimately securing victory through a blend of precision shots and strategic placements.

Impact and Reflection:
Winning the Zonal Table Tennis Championship organized by Shivaji University was not just a personal victory, but a tribute to the collective effort of my team, coaches, and supporters. This achievement reinforced my belief in the power of determination and hard work. It also underlined the importance of discipline, focus, and sportsmanship in the realm of competitive sports.

Conclusion:
Participating in and triumphing at the Zonal Table Tennis Championship was an experience that will forever be etched in my memory. This achievement has motivated me to continue pushing my boundaries, both on and off the table. As I look back on that exhilarating day, I am reminded that success is a culmination of passion, preparation, and perseverance.

TCS iON Career Edge

About TCS iON:
TCS iON is a strategic business unit of Tata Consultancy Services focused on enabling institutions, government departments and organisations from multiple industry sectors, to be efficient in their recruitment/admissions process, learning, skilling and overall business operations with the use of Phygital platforms. These are platforms that overlay digital technologies over physical assets.

About this course:
The job market today is fraught with challenges and is more competitive than ever before, requiring youth to put their best foot forward to win. If used smartly, this period can be a self-transformational stage for them. The need of the hour is for youth to be equipped with core employability skills such as communication, collaboration, business etiquette, financial and digital literacy skills to be gainfully employed. TCS iON Career Edge - Young Professional is a free to access 15-day career preparedness course that has been designed with the intent to equip the youth of today with core employability skills to take on the future.

Complete course comprise in fourteen modules each of them is very easy to grasp...
The modules includes as follows :-
1. Develop Soft Skills that Industry Demands.
2. Making Impactful Presentations.
3. Learn Corporate Etiquette.
4. Write an Effective Email.
5. Improve Interpersonal Skills for Better Results.
6. Prepare a Strong Resume and Cover Letter.
7. Learn Corporate Telephone Etiquette.
8. Understand How to Ace Corporate Interviews.
9. Be prepared for group Discussions.
10. Gain Guidance from Career Gurus.
11. Understand Accounting Fundamentals.
12. Gain Foundational Skills in IT.
13. Understand Artificial Intelligence (AI) - Part 1.
14. Understand Artificial Intelligence (AI) - Part 2
